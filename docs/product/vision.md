# Product Vision

## Goal

PlaySharp helps players build a daily learning habit around poker and blackjack with short
interactive drills, immediate correction, and clear progression tracking.

## Audience

- Beginners who need structure
- Intermediate players who want to reduce recurring mistakes
- Competitive players who want repeatable practice and measurable progress

## Core loop

1. Pick a game or receive the daily quiz
2. Answer a short scenario-based question
3. Get immediate feedback and an explanation
4. Review weak themes and continue practicing

## V1 scope

- Poker and blackjack
- Daily quiz flow
- Lesson library
- Progress tracking
- Integrated read-only content administration

## Out of scope for V1

- Premium subscription and Stripe
- Social leagues and rankings
- Advanced analytics
